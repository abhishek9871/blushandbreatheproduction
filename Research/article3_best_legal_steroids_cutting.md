## How to Maximize Cutting Results (Diet + Training)

Supplements alone won't get you shredded. Here's how to maximize your cutting phase:

### Nutrition Fundamentals

**Caloric Deficit:** Aim for 300-500 calories below maintenance. Too aggressive leads to muscle loss.

**Protein Intake:** 1-1.2g per pound of bodyweight to preserve muscle. Non-negotiable during cutting.

**Carb Timing:** Place most carbs around workouts for energy and recovery.

**Hydration:** Minimum 1 gallon (3.8L) daily. Helps with fat metabolism and reduces water retention.

### Training Approach

**Keep Lifting Heavy:** Don't switch to "light weights, high reps" during cutting. Maintain intensity to signal your body to keep muscle.

**Strategic Cardio:**
- 3-4 sessions of LISS (Low-Intensity Steady State) per week
- 1-2 sessions of HIIT (High-Intensity Interval Training)
- Don't overdo it — excessive cardio can burn muscle

**When to Take Supplements:**
- **Clenbutrol:** 30 minutes before workout
- **Anvarol:** 15 minutes after workout
- **Winsol:** With main meal
- **Testo-Max:** 20 minutes before breakfast
