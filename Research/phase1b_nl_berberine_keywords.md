# Phase 1B: Dutch (NL-BE) Berberine + Ozempic Alternative Keyword Research

**Research Date:** December 4, 2025  
**Target Regions:** Belgium (Vlaanderen) & Netherlands  
**Language:** Dutch (Nederlands)  
**Research Methodology:** Real SERP analysis via Tavily search (google.be/google.nl simulation)

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This research analyzed 15 Dutch-language keywords related to berberine and Ozempic alternatives for the Belgian and Dutch markets. Key findings:

- **High commercial intent** exists across buying-related keywords
- **Affiliate-friendly SERP landscape** with mix of e-commerce, pharmacies, and health blogs
- **Belgian market** has stronger pharmacy presence (Medibib, Newpharma, Pazzox, Farmaline)
- **Dutch market** has more supplement retailers (Holland & Barrett, Vitaminstore, Bol.com)
- **Test-Aankoop.be** (Belgian consumer organization) ranks highly for informational queries
- **Quick-win opportunities** identified for 6 keywords with low competition
