> **"Vous cherchez l'efficacité de l'Ozempic sans ordonnance? GLP Lab combine berbérine nouvelle génération, chrome et extraits synergiques pour activer naturellement votre GLP-1 — là où la berbérine seule échoue avec seulement 5% d'absorption et des résultats décevants."**

This sentence works because it:
1. **Hijacks high-intent traffic** from Ozempic/GLP-1 medication seekers
2. **Attacks standalone Berberine's documented weakness** (poor 5-10% absorption rate)
3. **Positions multi-ingredient formula as the solution** to known consumer frustrations
4. **Creates urgency through comparison** — complete formula vs. incomplete solution

### Supporting bridge arguments for content body

The French Berberine market research revealed these specific consumer pain points to exploit:

- **Absorption problem**: "La berbérine brute se caractérise par une très mauvaise biodisponibilité" — standard berberine has only 5-10% absorption
- **Slow, modest results**: UFC-Que Choisir reports only ~3kg weight loss with high doses
- **GI side effects**: Diarrhea, nausea, and abdominal pain frequently reported
- **Dosage confusion**: Studies recommend 900-1500mg/day but French law caps supplements at 360mg/day
- **Incomplete solution**: Best results require adding chrome and silymarin — consumers must buy multiple products

The bridge strategy: Position GLP Lab as the "formule complète" that solves what "berbérine seule" cannot.
